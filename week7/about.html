About Davis Family Medicine

We began serving the community in 2018. The Davis's have all have degrees in their specific fields from Pediatircs to Podiatry. After separately working for different pratices and hospitals, they decided to come together as a family to work for the community. They offer a variety of services such as:

  • Physical Exams
  • Vaccines
  • Diagnosis and Treatment of Chronic Diseases
  • Illnesses
  • Minor Procedures
  • Lab Services
  • Women’s, Men’s, and Children’s Health Services
